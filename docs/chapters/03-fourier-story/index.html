The problem: heat in a metal bar

Fourier's bold claim

Fourier proposed that any function — no matter how complicated — could be written as an infinite sum of sines and cosines: $$f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[ a_n \cos\!\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) + b_n \sin\!\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) \right]$$
